What it does

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Network Blocking

Blocks ads, scripts, and tracking domains at the network level using Chrome's Declarative Net Request API. Fast, efficient, and battery-friendly.

Cosmetic Filtering

Hides ad containers, banners, and sponsored content with CSS selector-based filtering. Add your own custom selectors for full control.

Cookie Consent Hiding

Dismisses cookie banners, consent modals, and page-locking overlays automatically so you can get straight to the content.

Tracking Protection

Blocks analytics and tracking scripts from Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Microsoft Clarity, comScore, and Bing telemetry.

Notification Blocking

Blocks annoying site notification permission requests. No more pop-ups asking to send you alerts.

Video Ad Blocking

Blocks pre-roll video ads by intercepting the Google IMA SDK on supported sites.

Per-Site Control

Pause blocking on any site from the popup. Add exact hostnames or wildcard patterns to your allowlist.

Auto-Updating Rules

Filter lists sync silently in the background from a secure API, so new ad patterns are caught without waiting for an extension update.
