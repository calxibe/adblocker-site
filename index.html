What it does

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Network Blocking

Blocks common ad and tracking domains using Chrome's Declarative Net Request API. Fast, efficient, and battery-friendly.

Cosmetic Filtering

Hides ad containers, banners, and sponsored content with CSS selector-based filtering. Pages look cleaner instantly.

Cookie Consent Hiding

Dismisses cookie banners, consent modals, and page-locking overlays automatically so you can get straight to the content.

Video Ad Blocking

Blocks pre-roll video ads by intercepting the Google IMA SDK on supported sites.

Per-Site Control

Pause blocking on any site directly from the popup. Add wildcard patterns to your allowlist from the options page.

Custom Rules

Add your own CSS selectors, block domains, and video ad domains. Full control from a clean settings page.
